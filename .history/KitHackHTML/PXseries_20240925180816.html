PX series

The series of planes that kicks off Pamishly's kithack designs


PX-01 Twinsies

As the first plane to the PX-series it is by far the worst. It can't manuever and it really sucks with speed. But it's not to be judge as it is the beggining of it all

PX-02 Splinter

The second plane in the series but the first to start showing the traditional fighter jet style. Not the greatest with speed or maneuverability but a definite upgrade from the 01

PX-03 Scepter

Moving over to one engine design, the Ripper offers much better maneuverability and speed than the Splinter however, the interesting wing design makes it feel too long.

PX-04 Ravager

Earning the name Ravager from its high speed and maneuverability, this plane is the first of a the next generation of PX planes, however it has an issue with the batteries

PX-05 Ripper

While not as fast as the Raver, this plane is still registered as an upgrade due to its immense maneuverability advantage, being the first to properly pull a cobra manmaneuver.

PX-06 Stardrive

Significantly faster than any previos plane in the series, while it loses some maneuverability it is fast enough and can run off one out of two engines, so it takes the place as 06.

PX-07 Singularity

This thing cobras so easily, while it is slightly slower than the Stardrive it can dodge bullets so quick.

PX-08 Pulsar

Not the worst plane but the 07 is better, the battery life is garbage and it doesnt manuever the greatest however it is faster.

PX-09 Horizon

The first plane in the series to have moving wings and not just the first to have moving landing gear, but first to have any landing gear in the first place.

PX-10 Arrowhead

It doesn't have any fancy hydraulics but its got them front swept wings and looks sick, also really fun to stand on its engines, not a hover but its still fun.

PX-11 Cobra

Back to the hydraulics with the retractalble landing gear, while the wings dont move on this one, it does have airbrakes that extend out the side, giving it its name the Cobra.
