Beamng.drive Dune Configs

All cars work without mods however some may have some optional features with certain mods installed


Scintilla

This abomination of a super car is great for the dunes and has an interesting look, with the engine it comes with it is an amazing driver.

Bolide

The car before the Scintilla. while it lacks AWD it still has the necessary power for the dunes. However, still an abomination of a super car.

Aurata

A Side-by-Side that can certainly take a hit. Should still drive even after a roll or two. It can handle a better engine, however it makes it less drivable so a downgrade was necessary.

Dune Kicker

Honestly this doesn't deserve a description, worst thing on this list, contrary to the name.

ETK-800

While better than the Dune Kicker, the wheel size difference on this thing even confuses me and I made it. Performs good enough to be shared though so I'll take it.

H-Series

This was made as a joke but works surprisingly well that I had to keep it. Also if you install the Company Cruiser livery mod, this thing uses that.

Old Pessima

This is for the older Pessima model, this thing performs as well as it looks, as in its up for opinion I honestly can't tell you how well it works.

Piccolina

This is my favorite one and I don't even like how it looks, enough said.

Roamer

I would love to do this to my Dad's Suburban, this thing works wonderfully and it is so much fun to drive, oh and it can really take a beating without flinching.
